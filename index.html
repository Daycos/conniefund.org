The Connie Fund exists to benefit the children of Northeast Nebraska in the counties of Madison, Antelope, Boone, Pierce, Platte, Stanton, and Wayne.

The Connie Fund focuses on strategic funding of projects, programs, and initiatives that promote philanthropy, well-being, and a brighter future of the youth of this area.

Grants will only be awarded to other 501 (c)(3) corporations or state or local government agencies. Applications are accepted on an ongoing basis.

For more information, please contact the Nebraska Community Foundation or (402) 323-7330.
